The Darkness

Survive a city taken over by the Dark Night. Fight off shape-shifting enemies using spirit points earned in battle. Level up to unlock legendary weapons like the Darkness Sword, Glock 80, and more. Each level gets harder, but with upgrades like teleportation, time-warping skateboards, and darkness bullets — you might stand a chance.

💥 Leveling XP: 5,000 XP (Lv 1-5), increasingly difficult beyond.
🧪 Spirit Points: Earn by defeating enemies
🗡️ Weapons: Darkness Sword, Glock 80, Demon Slayer Rifle, Shadow Katana, Time Skater

Goal: Reach Level 100 to defeat Dark Night and restore peace.
